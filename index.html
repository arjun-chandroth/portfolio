// About Me

Building the
Future of Robotics.

I am a B.Tech Mechatronics Engineering student specializing in robotic systems, autonomous platforms, AI-based perception, and full-stack robot integration.

I focus on building scalable robotics architectures where a single system controls multiple robot platforms — humanoids, hexapods, quadrupeds, and mobile robots — all unified under one intelligent control layer.

I have hands-on experience with ROS2, YOLO-based vision, OpenCV, LIDAR visualization, GPS mapping, multi-camera systems, and remote robotic access. My background in digital art further strengthens my system visualization and design skills.
